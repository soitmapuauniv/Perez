About

An Information Systems (IS) major is great for students who enjoy computer science, business, and technology. It is an excellent option if you are fascinated by how these areas collaborate. This major is ideal for you if you would like to connect technology to benefit people and make businesses better.

Source


Information Systems teaches us on how technology, processes, and people are utilized to transform data into information. It addresses hardware, software, and networks that assist in gathering, processing, and analyzing information for decision-making.

Source


Information Systems emphasize employing technology to improve work processes and solve business problems. With a degree in Information Systems, you are taught to manage technology systems for businesses, public organizations, and non-profits.
